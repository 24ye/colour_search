# colour_search

An attempt to vibe code a tool that lets you find youtube songs by searching the dominant color of their thumbnails.

Essentially asks user for a colour, analyzes thumbnail jpg images based on the specificed colour, and returns thumbnails that are primarly composed of that colour.

I have a ton of songs in my youtube playlists that I sometimes forget their video titles, or some video titles have a language other than english (or symbols), so I can't search them up using a regular english keyboard. Howeever, I find it easier to remember what their thumbnails looked like, or at least the general colour of their thumbnails. 
The motivation behind this tool was to not get stumpped the next time I try to search a song I want to listen to but forgot the name of the song / can't input the video title in the search bar.
